About

Where rhythm meets logic

I'm Andy — a bass player with a deep love for groove, and a software engineer with a passion for elegant systems. I bring the same creative discipline to both worlds.

With over a decade of performing and teaching, I've helped hundreds of students find their voice on the bass. In the studio, I build modern web applications, APIs, and AI-integrated systems that solve real problems. The bridge between these worlds is pattern recognition — the same instinct that helps a student nail a walking bassline helps me architect clean, scalable code.
