About

Melbourne bassist,
teacher & lifer

I've been playing bass for over four decades, across original bands, session work, and live touring. My main weapon is a beat-up Fender Precision that's been on more stages than I can count.

I teach all styles and levels from my studio in Melbourne, with a focus on feel, timing, and musicality over flash. I believe every bassist finds their own voice — my job is to help you find yours faster.
