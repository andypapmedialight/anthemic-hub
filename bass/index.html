About

Melbourne bassist,
teacher & lifer

OK, so Dewey Finn was not technically correct in this quote (cello's are tuned differently), but his vibe is bang on. Same with me!

I've been playing bass for four decades, across original bands, session work, and live touring. My main weapon is a beat-up Fender Precision that's been on more stages than I can count.

I teach all styles and levels from my studio in Melbourne, with a focus on feel, timing, and musicality, and FUN. I believe every bassist can find their own THANG — my job is to help you find yours faster.
